# VoxType - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 29, 2026**

## Overview

VoxType ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What VoxType Does:

- ✅ Processes voice input locally in your browser
- ✅ Uses Google's Web Speech API for transcription (handled by your browser)
- ✅ Stores user preferences locally using Chrome's storage API
- ✅ Copies transcribed text to your clipboard (only when you use the feature)

### What VoxType Does NOT Do:

- ❌ Does not collect personal information
- ❌ Does not track your usage
- ❌ Does not send data to external servers (except browser's Speech API)
- ❌ Does not use analytics or tracking tools
- ❌ Does not share data with third parties

## Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions:

1. **Microphone Access**: Required to capture your voice for transcription
   - Processed locally by your browser's Web Speech API
   - No audio is stored or transmitted by VoxType

2. **Clipboard Write**: Allows auto-copying transcribed text to clipboard
   - Only activated when you use the copy feature
   - No clipboard data is read or stored

3. **Storage**: Saves your preferences locally
   - All data stays on your device
   - No synchronization with external servers

4. **Side Panel**: Displays the VoxType interface
   - No data collection involved

## Third-Party Services

VoxType uses your browser's built-in **Web Speech API**, which is provided by Google. When you use voice transcription:

- Audio is processed by Google's speech recognition service
- This is handled directly by your Chrome browser
- Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers operated by VoxType.

## Children's Privacy

VoxType does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.
